# 🚀 BulkReach Pro: The Complete Guide

Welcome to **BulkReach Pro**, your premium command center for high-converting bulk email outreach. This guide will walk you through setting up your account and launching your first professional campaign.

---

## 🛠️ Step 1: Essential Setup
Before sending any emails, you must configure your mail server credentials.

1.  **Navigate to Settings**: Click "Settings" in the sidebar.
2.  **SMTP (Sending)**: 
    *   Enter your SMTP Host (e.g., `smtp.gmail.com`) and Port (usually `465` for SSL or `587` for TLS).
    *   Use an **App Password** if you use Gmail/Outlook.
    *   Click **"Test SMTP Connection"** to verify.
3.  **IMAP (Replies)**: 
    *   Configure this to track when recipients reply to your emails.
    *   Click **"Test IMAP Connection"** to verify.
4.  **AI (Gemini)**:
    *   Add your Google Gemini API Key to enable **AI Smart Compose**.

---

## ✍️ Step 2: Crafting Your Message
Go to the **Bulk Mail** page to start composing.

*   **Templates**: Select from the **"Load Template..."** dropdown to use one of the 10+ professional outreach starters.
*   **AI Smart Compose**: Fill out your goal, tone, and value proposition on the right panel, then click **"Generate Draft"**. The AI will write a personalized message for you.
*   **Personalization**: Use tokens like `{first_name}` and `{company}` in your subject or body. They will be automatically replaced with the recipient's data.
*   **Spam Check**: Keep an eye on the **Spam Score** meter. Avoid words like "FREE", "WINNER", or "URGENT" to ensure high deliverability.

---

## 👥 Step 3: Managing Recipients
In the **Recipients** tab, you have four ways to add targets:

1.  **Saved List**: Select a list you've previously imported.
2.  **Manual Input**: Paste a raw list of emails (one per line). Great for quick, small tests.
3.  **Upload CSV**: Drag and drop a file. The system automatically detects Name and Organization columns.
4.  **Google Sheets**: Connect a live sheet for dynamic outreach.

> **TIP**: Always click **"Validate All Emails"** before sending to remove "dead" or "invalid" addresses and protect your sender reputation.

---

## 🚀 Step 4: Launching & Monitoring
Once your message and recipients are ready, go to **Schedule & Send**.

*   **Campaign Name**: Give it a unique name for your history.
*   **Send Delay**: We recommend **5-10 minutes** between emails to look like a human and avoid spam filters.
*   **Launch**: Click **🚀 Launch Campaign**.
*   **Controls**: Once running, use the **Pause** or **Cancel** buttons in the top header if you need to stop the engine.
*   **Live Feed**: Watch the "Live Activity Feed" at the bottom to see real-time logs of every email sent.

---

## 📥 Step 5: Tracking Replies
The **Replies** page acts as your unified inbox.

*   **Sync Now**: Click this to fetch the latest replies from your IMAP server.
*   **Thread Tracking**: Replies are automatically linked to the specific campaign that started the conversation.

---

## 👑 Admin Features (For Management)
If you are an administrator, you have extra powers:

*   **User Management**: Create and manage accounts for your team.
*   **Impersonation**: Click the 👁️ icon next to any user to see the dashboard exactly as they see it.
*   **Live Monitor**: A global view of every email being sent across the entire platform in real-time.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Success
*   **Warm up**: Start with small batches (20-50 per day) and gradually increase.
*   **A/B Testing**: Save multiple templates to see which subject lines get the most replies.
*   **Clean Lists**: Use the **Contacts** page to regularly delete "Invalid" or "Risky" emails.
