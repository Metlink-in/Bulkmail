🚀 1. Getting Started

Before you can send emails, you need to connect your email account via SMTP.

1

Go to Settings → Sender Profiles

Click Settings in the sidebar, then click + Add Profile. Enter your SMTP credentials — host, port, username, and password.

2

Common SMTP Settings

Gmail: host smtp.gmail.com, port 587, STARTTLS ✓ — use an App Password.
Outlook: host smtp.office365.com, port 587, STARTTLS ✓.
Custom SMTP: check with your email provider.

3

Test Your Connection

After saving, click Test on the profile card. A green success message with latency confirms it works. Fix any errors before sending campaigns.

💡 Tip: Set one profile as Default — it will be used automatically for all campaigns unless you pick a different one.
👥 2. Contacts

Contacts are organized into lists. A single contact can belong to multiple lists.

1

Import from CSV

Go to Contacts → click Import CSV. Your CSV should have these columns (headers are case-insensitive):

email, name, org (company), custom_1, custom_2, custom_3

2

Paste Emails Directly

Paste a list of email addresses (one per line or comma-separated). They'll be added with their email prefix as the name.

3

Manage Lists

Create named lists (e.g. "Q2 Leads", "Webinar Attendees"). Each list shows contact count. You can delete individual contacts or entire lists.

⚠️ Important: Make sure your contact's name field is filled in, not left as "user" or the email prefix. This is what replaces {first_name} in your emails.
✉️ 3. Templates & Merge Tags

Templates are reusable email designs. BulkReach includes 9 professional templates to start with.

1

Browse Global Templates

Go to Templates. Global templates (marked with a star) are available to all users. Click Preview to see how they look, or Use Template to copy it as your own.

2

Edit a Template

Open a template in the editor. Use the Visual tab for point-and-click editing, HTML tab for raw code, and CSS tab for custom styles. Changes auto-preview on the right.

3

Use Merge Tags

Place these anywhere in the subject or body — they get replaced per recipient when sending:

{first_name} {last_name} {full_name} {company} {org} {email} {custom_1} {custom_2} {custom_3}
💡 If a contact has no name, {first_name} will use their email prefix automatically (e.g. john from john@example.com).
📤 4. Bulk Mail Campaigns

Send a template to a saved contact list. Each email is sent individually with personalized merge tags.

1

Go to Bulk Mail

Select your Contact List, pick a Template (or write a custom subject + body), choose your SMTP Profile, and set a delay between sends.

2

Set Delay & Daily Limit

A delay of 120–300 seconds between emails helps avoid spam filters. The daily limit (default 500) prevents your account from being flagged by your email provider.

3

Monitor Progress

After launching, a live progress bar shows sent / failed counts. The page polls every few seconds. If you navigate away, you can check History to see the job's status.

⚠️ Gmail tip: Use an App Password (not your main password). Enable 2FA on your Google account first, then create an App Password at myaccount.google.com/apppasswords.
🎯 5. Custom Outreach

Send hyper-personalized one-off emails with custom name & company data — no contact list needed.

📋 Paste or Upload CSV Include columns: email, first_name, last_name, company. Auto-detected from headers.
✍️ Compose with Merge Tags Click merge tag chips to insert them at cursor. Preview resolves them live for any recipient.
👁️ Live Preview Pick any recipient from the dropdown and see exactly how their email will look before sending.
📊 Track Progress Real-time progress bar, sent/failed counts, current recipient, and next-send countdown.
💡 When to use Custom Outreach vs Bulk Mail: Use Custom Outreach when you have a fresh list with names & companies (e.g. LinkedIn exports, Apollo.io exports). Use Bulk Mail for your saved contact lists and recurring campaigns.
🗓️ 6. Schedules

Automate recurring campaigns — run a template to a contact list daily, weekly, or at a specific time.

1

Create a Schedule

Click + New Schedule. Enter a name, pick a template and contact list, choose the frequency (Once / Daily / Weekly), and set the run time.

2

Pause & Resume

Toggle the switch next to any schedule to pause or resume it. Paused schedules won't run until reactivated.

3

Calendar View

The calendar at the top shows when your scheduled campaigns are next due to run. Dots on a date indicate scheduled jobs.

💬 7. Reply Tracking

See who replied to your campaigns and read their messages without leaving the app.

1

Set Up IMAP

In Settings → Sender Profile, fill in the IMAP section (host, port, username, password). BulkReach checks your inbox periodically and imports replies.

2

Read Replies

Go to Replies. Unread replies show a badge on the sidebar. Click any reply to read the full message. Mark as read to keep your inbox organized.

💡 The unread reply count appears as a badge on the sidebar Replies link and on the Dashboard.
📊 8. History & Analytics

Track every campaign and see per-email delivery results.

📋 Campaign List See all campaigns with total / sent / failed counts and status badges.
🔍 Per-Email Logs Click any campaign row to expand and see each recipient's delivery status and message ID.
🔎 Filter & Search Filter by status (completed / running / failed) or search by campaign name.
📥 Export CSV Download campaign data as a CSV for external reporting or analysis.
