📬 Sending Best Practices

⏱
Use Delays

180–600 s between emails avoids spam filters and protects sender reputation.

📊
Respect Daily Limits

Gmail: ~500/day. G Suite: ~2000/day. SendGrid/custom SMTP: as configured.

🎨
Personalise

Emails using {first_name} and {company} tags get 26% higher open rates.

🔥
Warm Up First

New accounts: start with 20–50 emails/day and increase gradually over 2 weeks.
